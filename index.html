Meet $AWETISM a little germ with a big problem—he’s not the brightest microbe in the petri dish! While his buddies are busy splitting and thriving, Benny often forgets which way to swim, mistakes sugar for salt, and gets stuck in the same spot for hours. His flagella might be spinning, but Benny’s definitely not winning any bacterial IQ contests!


$AWETISM was born in a top-secret laboratory where scientists were experimenting with gene splicing to create super-intelligent bacteria. Something went hilariously wrong, and instead of genius-level germs, out popped $AWETISM . From day one, he struggled with basic tasks: mistaking his tail for food, bumping into walls, and generally being the microbial equivalent of a lovable klutz. Despite his mishaps, $AWETISM's cheerful spirit and endless curiosity made him a favorite among the lab staff, proving that even a baffled bacterium can bring a lot of joy to the world!
